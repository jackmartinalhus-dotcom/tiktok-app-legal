Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 18, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the "Auto Post" application (the "App") handles information. The App is a personal tool that publishes videos to the account holder's own TikTok account using TikTok's official Content Posting API.

1. Information We Handle

To function, the App uses an access token and refresh token issued by TikTok when you authorize your account. These credentials are stored only on your own computer in a local configuration file. The App also processes the video files and captions you choose to upload.

2. How Information Is Used

Credentials are used solely to authenticate with TikTok and publish videos that you explicitly request. Video files are uploaded only to your own TikTok account.

3. Data Storage and Sharing

The App runs locally on your device. It does not operate a server, does not transmit your data to the developer, and does not sell or share your information with any third party other than TikTok, as required to perform the upload you request.

4. Data Retention

Tokens remain in your local configuration file until you delete them or revoke the App's access in your TikTok account settings. Downloaded video files are temporary and removed after upload unless you choose to keep them.

5. Your Choices

You can revoke the App's access at any time from your TikTok account's "Manage app permissions" settings, and you can delete the local configuration file to remove stored credentials.

6. Third-Party Services

Use of TikTok's API is governed by TikTok's own Privacy Policy. The App is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by TikTok or ByteDance.
